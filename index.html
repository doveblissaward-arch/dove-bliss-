About DOBA 2025

The Dove Bliss Award 2025 (DOBA2025), presented by Dove Bliss Company—a dynamic media and events firm based in Abuja and branch office in Abakaliki, Ebonyi State, Nigeria—stands as a beacon of recognition for merit-driven achievements. As the biggest award event in the history of Ebonyi State with proven track records since its inception in 2019, DOBA has consistently honored exemplary individuals while fostering youth empowerment and community development.

Dove Bliss Award 2025: The Excellent Path

Held annually at the prestigious International Conference Centre in Abakaliki, this event has evolved into a cornerstone platform for celebrating men and women of impeccable reputation and impactful contributions across technology, business, leadership and governance, education, entrepreneurship, and entertainment. Notably, DOBA2025 is NOT A VOTING CATEGORY —it is purely merit-based, ensuring awards are determined by a rigorous, impartial evaluation of achievements rather than public opinion or influence, reinforcing its commitment to integrity and excellence.
